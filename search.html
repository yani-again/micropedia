SOME NOTES!

  1. This website exclusively focuses on STM32 microcontrollers. If you are using something else, take this information with a grain of salt. If you are using something with a Cortex-M processor, there is still valuable information you can extract, though not every article will be applicable to a non-STM32 microcontroller.
  2. I'm a university student and this site is my passive personal project. I collect my notes as I learn bare-metal embedded programming, then I tidy and upload them here. Being in the learning phase, there are likely a bunch of mistakes around the site. If you spot any, please let me know!
